"Taking an image, freezing a moment, reveals how rich reality truly is."

This anonymous quote was my inspiration to pick up photography as a hobby. Based in Leipzig, my interest extends beyond macro photography to the colours of nature and the busy streets, essentially attempting to capture what goes unnoticed in our everyday life.

I currently shoot with a Canon 100D and a kit lens (18-55mm). Check out my Instagram for more clicks, and contact me by email!
